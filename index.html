Ride To Vote

We recognize that the center of our democracy is the right to vote. We also understand that not everyone can get to the polls to exercise that right. This excludes and disenfranchises those people from using the rights guaranteed to them by the constitution of these United States.

This site is dedicated to helping resolve that problem by providing transportation to those who need it and providing an independent witness to any issues encountered while trying to cast your ballot.

Please stay tuned while we build out this platform. We are hoping to have this ready for you before the 2018 elections. We greatly appreciate your patience
